The Process

From intake to delivery
in four stages.

01
Scope
A $300 discovery call scopes the engagement. We research your operation before we pick up the phone. You leave with a written proposal and a full scoping document. The $300 credits in full if you sign.
02
Source
You send what you have — job descriptions, SOPs, knowledge base exports, training materials, CRM workflows. We review everything and identify the highest-frequency call types and the most critical failure points.
03
Build
We build the guide situation by situation. Every situation gets the full Five-Operation Panel: case setup, two-column steps, agent scripts, hard stops, and case note templates. Draft delivered in two to three weeks.
04
Deliver
Client reviews via a recorded walkthrough. One round of revisions included. Final guide delivered as a single HTML file — host anywhere. No additional software required. Live on day one.
