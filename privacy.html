2. What Data We Collect and Why

We collect only what is necessary to operate these websites and respond to inquiries. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes — ever.

2.01 Contact Form Submissions (rudolphcx.com)

When you submit a contact form on rudolphcx.com, we collect your name, email address, message content, and any additional information you choose to provide. Purpose: To respond to your inquiry. Legal basis (GDPR): Legitimate interest. Retention: 24 months from submission or resolution.

2.02 Website Analytics — Plausible Analytics (both sites)

Both sites use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-preserving analytics tool. Plausible collects aggregated data only: page views, referral sources, browser type, country-level geography, and time on page. Plausible does not set cookies, collect personal data, store IP addresses, or track individuals across sites or devices. No consent banner is required. See plausible.io/data-policy.

2.03 FirstStep Guide Usage (getfirststep.org)

The FirstStep guide does not require registration, login, or any personal information. No user data is collected through guide interactions. The guide runs entirely in your browser.

2.04 Email Communications

If you contact us directly by email, we collect your email address and the content of your message, used solely to respond to your communication.

2.05 Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect payment information, Social Security numbers, government IDs, biometric data, health or medical information, or data from children under 13.
